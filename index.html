Reaper

A multipurpose discord bot matching all your needs.
What is Reaper Capable of?

Reaper is a multipurpose discord bot, with salient features such as Moderation, Music, Meme, Jokes, Game Stats and more.

Moderation

Moderation commands such as warn, kick, ban, mute, and more coming up soon.

Music

Search and play for your favourite music in discord itself.

24/7 Uptime

The Bot has a 24/7 Uptime being up 99.99% of time unless there is a little downtime for maintenance or bug fixes.

Setup Server Command

The setup server command is an unique feature in this bot. Extremely Helpful. You are new to discord? Want to create a new server? Just use $setupserver and voila the bot will automatically create channels and roles for you

Game Stats Commands

Get your in-game statistics and performance for Games like Fortnite and osu! within discord itself. Just do the command and enter your username, and Reaper will present your stats in front of you.

Image Commands

 
Fun image commands, that use your's or the mentioned user's avatar to create funny pics or memes. We keep make these commands so that they are easy and fun to use. Go on use these commands and make memes
